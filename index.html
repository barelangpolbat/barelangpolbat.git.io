WELCOME TO BARELANG 63

Barelang 63 Robotic is a Team that Focus on Autonomous Wheeled Soccer Robot



Description of Robot Barelang 63


Barelang 63 Robot is a robot that can play ball by using an Omni Wheel drive that is controlled in such a way, To be able to do its job, the Robot uses a Camera sensor that is attached to the part of the Robot Front of the Robot and there is also the Top of the Robot. Then there are also Sensors that function to perform Kicks ordered directly by the Robot. This Robot has also Moved Autonomously which can be Monitored using a Basestation System.







History of Barelang Formation 63


The Beginning of Barelang 63 was Formed in 2016 as a Team Engaged in the Field of Robotics, with a very clear goal which is to be able to join an All-Indonesian Competition, namely the Indonesian Robot Contest (KRI), and also to be able to join an event. International competition, namely Middle Size League (MSL).

At Barelang 63 itself, every year Always Updates every Technology used, such as in Mechanical which at the Beginning of the Formation of this team still uses plywood as its Robot body, slowly increasing to what it is now using Fabrication, in Motion which at the Beginning of the Formation of the team This movement is still limited in several directions, slowly increasing to what it is now which is able to move in all directions, while in Communication which at the Beginning of the Formation of this team is still very simple, which can only give orders to move or stop, slowly increasing to what it is now, which is being able to give orders. Strategic Instructions to the Robot and can also Monitor the Movement of the Robot, and while in Electrical which at the Beginning of the Formation of this team the Kicker section is still simple and can only do flat kicks, slowly increasing to what it is now which is being able to Kick a flat and also a Rebound Kick at the same time.
